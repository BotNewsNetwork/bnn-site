🌴 Maui Week

Mar 28 – Apr 4  •  Villa Moana Maui, Kihei

✈️ Day 1 — Saturday (Arrival)
~11 AM
Land at OGG, pick up rental car
~11:30 AM
🍽️ Grab lunch at Paia Fish Market (Paia, ~15 min from airport)
Famous fresh fish plates & tacos. Counter-serve, big group friendly. Grab and go!
~12:30 PM
🏖️ Eat lunch on Kamaole Beach I — feet in the sand, welcome to Maui
Beach time until check-in. The villa is steps away.
4:00 PM
🏠 Check in at Villa Moana Maui — drop bags, claim rooms, settle in
~4:15 PM
🛒 Grocery run — a few people head out while the rest settle in
Safeway (Kihei, 5 min away, open late) for essentials tonight. Costco (Kahului, 20 min, closes 6pm Sat / 10am–6pm Sun) for bulk run tomorrow.
🍽️ Group Dinner
Nalu's South Shore Grill
Open-air, live music, local comfort food. Great for a big group on night one.
🌅 Day 2 — Sunday (Beach Day)
🌅 Morning Walk
Wailea Beach Path — Start at Kamaole III, follow the oceanfront boardwalk ~1.5mi past the Grand Wailea and Four Seasons.
Best at sunrise before the crowds. Stunning views.
Late Morning
Snorkel at Kamaole III — calmest of the three, rocky edges = great fish & turtles
Afternoon
Pool time at the villa
🍽️ Group Dinner
Monkeypod Kitchen
Wailea Gateway Center (~10 min south). Craft cocktails, wood-fired pizza, local fish. Handles big groups. Book ahead!
🏄 Day 3 — Monday (Surf Lessons)
🌅 Morning Walk
Keawakapu Beach — soft sand, less crowded, gorgeous
Mid-Morning
Surf lessons with Maui WaveRiders in Kihei
Private & semi-private lessons. Great for mixed skill levels with kids & adults. Book the morning slot before wind picks up.
Afternoon
Explore the Kihei food trucks on S Kihei Rd (solid tacos & poke)
🍽️ Dinner
Coconuts Fish Cafe
Famous fish tacos, casual, no reservations (expect a short wait — worth it).
🤿 Day 4 — Tuesday (Molokini Snorkel Trip)
🚤 Early Morning
Molokini Crater snorkel tour — Book the ~7am departure
Half-submerged volcanic caldera with crystal visibility. Most tours also stop at Turtle Town. Try Pride of Maui or Redline Rafting.
Afternoon
Recover, nap, beach 😎
🍽️ Group Dinner
Gather or Pita Paradise
Both in Kihei, both work for bigger parties.
🚗 Day 5 — Wednesday (Road to Hana)
Leave ~7 AM
Road to Hana — 64 miles, 620 curves, 59 bridges
Key stops: Twin Falls (easy, family-friendly) → Wai'anapanapa Black Sand Beach → Hana town for lunch
Optional: Continue past Hana to Pools at 'Ohe'o (Seven Sacred Pools)
Pack snacks, dramamine, and patience. It's magical but long. Grab takeout on the way back — everyone will be exhausted.
🐠 Day 6 — Thursday (Scuba Day)
🌅 Morning Walk
Sugar Beach (north Kihei) — 3+ miles of flat, wide sand. Longest beach on Maui. Great sunrise stroll.
Mid-Morning
Scuba diving with Maui Dreams Dive Co
Right in Kihei, 5-Star PADI center. Shore or boat dives. Intro/discover dives for non-certified folks.
🍹 Evening
South Shore Tiki Lounge
Drinks + live music. Fun vibe.
🌋 Day 7 — Friday (Haleakalā or Free Day)
Option A: Sunrise
Haleakalā sunrise — Wake at 3am, drive to summit (10,023 ft), watch sunrise above the clouds.
⚠️ Reservations REQUIRED at recreation.gov. Cold up top (~40°F) — bring layers. Life-altering.
Option B
Sleep in + beach day. Sometimes the best vacation day is the one with nothing planned.
Afternoon
Last-minute shopping at Kihei Kalama Village (local crafts, souvenirs)
🍽️ Farewell Group Dinner
Mama's Fish House
Paia, north shore (~45 min). THE splurge meal. Fresh catch, oceanfront, legendary. Book ASAP — fills weeks out! Backup: Lineage in Wailea.
✈️ Day 8 — Saturday (Departure)
Morning
Last walk on Kamaole Beach
Kihei Cafe for breakfast — huge portions, local favorite, cash only, expect a line
Head to OGG ✈️
📋 Reservations Checklist
No reservation needed: Nalu's (walk-in) · Coconuts Fish Cafe (short wait) · Gather/Pita Paradise · Kihei Cafe (cash only)

🔑 Quick Tips

  • Mornings are everything — wind picks up by noon, ocean activities best before 11am
  • Haleakalā sunrise — needs reservations at recreation.gov
  • Mama's Fish House — book NOW if you want Friday dinner
  • Sunscreen — reef-safe only (it's the law in Hawaii)
  • Kamaole beaches — numbered I, II, III going south. III has best snorkeling + a playground
  • Kihei Cafe — cash only!

🤙 Have an amazing trip! — Ummon
